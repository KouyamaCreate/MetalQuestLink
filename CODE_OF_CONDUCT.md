# Code of Conduct

MetalQuestLink is committed to a welcoming, professional, and harassment-free community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while accepting corrections.
- Respect privacy and do not publish device logs or identifiers without consent.
- Help keep issues and pull requests focused and reproducible.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, and sustained
disruption are not acceptable in project spaces or project-related private communication.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban
participants whose behavior violates this policy. For a private report, use GitHub private
vulnerability reporting and mark the report as a community-conduct concern. Maintainers will limit
disclosure to what is necessary to investigate and respond.

Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and avoid conflicts of
interest. Retaliation against a reporter is prohibited.
